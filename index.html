Welcome to the Race Through Beskids 2023!

Are you ready to challenge yourself and push your limits? This race is not for the faint of heart, but for those who seek adventure and the thrill of cycling through the stunning Beskid Mountains.

Our self-supported race will take you through some of the most breathtaking scenery in Europe, as you pedal your way through the mountain passes and valleys, surrounded by forests, streams and wildlife.

You'll experience the raw beauty of the Beskid Mountains, as you tackle challenging climbs and exhilarating descents. But don't worry, you won't be alone. Our race attracts riders from all over the world, who share your passion for cycling and adventure.

The Race Through Beskid is not just a race, it's an experience. You'll meet new people, make lifelong friendships and create memories that will last a lifetime.

So, whether you're an experienced cyclist or a newcomer to the sport, come and join us for the ride of a lifetime. Register now and take your place in this epic challenge through the Beskid Mountains.
